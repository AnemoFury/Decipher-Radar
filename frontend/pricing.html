FAQs

How are payment fees calculated?
Our pricing is based on per-transaction fees. Each transaction that passes through Decipher Radar's fraud detection engine is charged a flat fee based on your plan. Volume discounts are available for businesses processing over $1M monthly.
Will I be charged tax?
Depending on your jurisdiction, applicable taxes may be added to your invoice. All listed prices are exclusive of tax.
Are there additional fees for refunds?
No, there are no additional fees for refunded transactions. However, the original screening fee is non-refundable as the fraud analysis has already been performed.
Do you have setup fees or monthly fees?
No setup fees. Our standard pricing model is purely usage-based with no minimum monthly charges. Enterprise plans may include a monthly platform fee in exchange for lower per-transaction rates.
Do you offer discounts?
Yes! We offer volume-based discounts for businesses with high transaction volumes. Startups may qualify for our startup programme with reduced rates. Contact our sales team for details.
