Privacy Policy

LAST_REVISION :: 2026.02.12

1. Data Collection

Decipher Radar collects transaction telemetry, device identifiers, and behavioral patterns to provide real-time fraud detection. We do not sell your data to third parties. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard protocols.

2. Purpose of Processing

The primary purpose of data processing is the identification and prevention of fraudulent financial activity. We use machine learning models trained on global network signals to distinguish between legitimate and malicious intent.

3. Compliance & Security

Our infrastructure is audited for SOC2 Type II compliance. We adhere to GDPR and CCPA regulations, providing users with the right to access and delete their data upon request.

4. Contact Infrastructure

For security concerns or data requests, contact our SOC at security@decipher-radar.io.
